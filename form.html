Info // Curatorial Statement

A meditation on craft — I make things for the web and screen that make people stop and ask “how was this built?”

I'm Denish Karki — 18, based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Self-taught. I learned by rebuilding the work that made me stare: 3D browser games, motion systems, and interfaces that feel alive.

Today I work across two fronts — real-time 3D & web on one side, content, video and graphic design on the other. A creative technologist who can design it, build it, and edit it. I care about the details most people scroll past.
